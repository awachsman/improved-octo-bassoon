"Unparalleled!"; "A true Makom Torah!"; "Inspiring!"; "Breathtaking grounds!"; "Menuchas hanefesh!"

These are some of the most often heard comments from visitors at Keser Torah Estates, located in Naugatuck CT.

Several years ago, the visionary leaders of Yeshivas Ohr Hachaim ,the renowned Yeshiva Gedola of Queens, embarked upon actualizing a long-held dream: to build a gated community that would be distinguished by a symbiotic relationship between home owners and a thriving mesivta and vibrant kollel.

Fast forward to today: Mesivta Yesodei Hatorah’s reputation as a premiere makom Torah has become widespread throughout the United States. Kolel Sifsei Dov has attracted a cadre of outstanding yungerleit who have created a ruach of ameilus baTorah. And new spacious, customized homes on large lots are under construction. Keser Torah Estates is well on its way to become the community of choice for Torah-minded mishpachos.
