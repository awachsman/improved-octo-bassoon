Kesser Torah Estates is a gated and self-contained residential community in the heart of Naugatuck, Connecticut. Plans call for construction of 62 single-family homes and 22 townhomes. The single family homes will be ituated on lots of varying sizes, up to approximately .7 acres. The Sales Map shown below provides an overview of the property. The accompanying Lot Parcel Table specifies acreage and square footage for the various lots.
